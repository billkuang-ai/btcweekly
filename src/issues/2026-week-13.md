    title: "Switzerland Registers Bitcoin-in-Reserves Constitutional Initiative; 100,000 Signatures Required by June 2026"
    summary: >-
      A constitutional initiative titled "Financially Strong, Sovereign, and Responsible Switzerland" has
      formally entered Switzerland's national referendum process, seeking to amend Article 99 of the Swiss
      Constitution to require the Swiss National Bank to hold Bitcoin alongside gold as a reserve asset.
      Organizers must collect 100,000 signatures within 18 months — with a campaign deadline effectively in
      mid-2026 — to qualify for a binding national vote. The initiative is co-led by Tether's vice
      president of energy and mining, Giw Zanganeh, and Swiss Bitcoin nonprofit 2B4CH. SNB Chairman Martin
      Schlegel has publicly opposed the proposal, citing price volatility.
